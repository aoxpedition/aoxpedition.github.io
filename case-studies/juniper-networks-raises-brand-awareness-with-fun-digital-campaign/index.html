Sourcing and Film
 Juniper Networks Raises Brand Awareness with Fun Digital Campaign

Overview

To facilitate greater brand awareness, Juniper Networks needed to create an inspiring and compelling video campaign that would introduce its products to a whole new generation of users. We created a series of three digital videos designed to raise brand awareness and highlight the technology behind many everyday moments –– making a video call, receiving a same-day delivery, and making a cup of coffee. We chose relatable scenarios that are only possible because of the incredible technical achievements that happen behind the scenes. We also consulted the internal Juniper team on the creation of a media plan.

The Challenge

  • Raise awareness and consideration of Juniper Networks

  • Showcase the everyday human moments that are only possible because of the technology designed by Juniper Networks

  • Create a series of three digital videos to show how the company’s top-notch technology solutions make everyday moments possible

The Strategy

  • We sought to humanize a tech brand by depicting the everyday relatable moments that they help foster. Our goal was to utilize depictions of everyday moments to capture the sentiment behind Juniper’s mission statement and showcase Juniper’s vast but often unseen influence on our lives.

  • We chose fun, short, snackable videos as the best format to position Juniper as more than just another tech company – their products and services help connect the world.

    • Motion graphics paired with human action are a clear but playful way to tell the story of how Juniper is part of our everyday life.

  • Xpedition provided concept ideation and full scale production for the “Engineering Simplicity” campaign, from scripting to casting to editing

  • Xpedition consulted on the internal marketing team’s media plan to guarantee the videos would reach—and resonate with—the right audience.

The Results

  • The campaign garnered 1M+ views on YouTube and was used in internal and external comms
